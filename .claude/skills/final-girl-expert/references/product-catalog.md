## Final Girl Core Box

- **Verification date:** 2026-03-28
- **Verification status:** Verified
- **Product type:** core box
- **Series:** core
- **Official URL:** https://vanrydergames.com/products/final-girl-core-box
- **Requires:** at least one Feature Film box
- **Official summary:** The Core Box contains the basic rules and all of the general components needed to play a game of Final Girl when combined with at least one Feature Film box. It is not playable by itself.
- **Exact component counts:**
  - 23 Action cards
  - 1 double-sided Player board
  - 6 custom dice
  - 3 Victim Holding boards and 3 matching tokens
  - 9 black and 2 white Final Health tokens

  - 2 Killer meeples / Horror markers
  - 1 Final Girl meeple
  - 8 tracking markers
  - 1 Time marker
  - 3 Special Victim meeples
  - 1 Bloodlust marker
  - 25 Health markers
  - Rulebook
- **Notes:** The official core rulebook confirms the modular system: if you own multiple Feature Films, you can pair any Killer with any Location.

  - Setup places facedown pill tokens on the board and one faceup beneath the Pill Effects card

  - This Feature Film adds Calm action cards that can turn Maniacs back into victims
  - Epic Dark Power "Shield of Madness" prevents the Ratchet Lady from losing health while any Maniacs are on the board
